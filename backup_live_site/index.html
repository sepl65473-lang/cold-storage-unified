ABOUT US

 At Smaatech Group, we are pioneers in sustainable engineering and diversified business ventures, driven by a passion for innovation and community impact. Founded in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, we blend cutting-edge technology with traditional expertise to deliver transformative solutions in water management, industrial automation, agritech, and emerging sectors. 

Our journey began with civil construction for water projects, evolving into a multifaceted conglomerate that supports Odisha's growth story. With a team of 50+ dedicated professionals—from ITI holders to MTech engineers—we prioritize quality, sustainability, and stakeholder value.

VISION

 To be Odisha's leading integrated solutions provider, fostering eco-friendly growth in water, agriculture, hospitality, education, and renewable energy.

MISSION

 Innovate relentlessly, empower communities, and build resilient infrastructure for generations to come.

KEY MILESSTONE

  • Completed 50+ water infrastructure projects across Eastern India.
  • Launched IoT-enabled agritech network serving 20+ villages.
  • Partnered with global leaders in automation and solar tech.
